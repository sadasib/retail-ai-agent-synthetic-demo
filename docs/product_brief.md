# Product Brief

## Problem
Retail AI assistants often look useful in demos but fail when the policy, product, or customer context is messy.

## Goal
Create a simple assistant framework that helps a product team decide when to answer, clarify, or escalate.

## Users
- AI PMs
- Product leaders
- UX designers
- Engineering leads
- Trust and safety partners

## Success criteria
- Clear decision policy
- Clean escalation logic
- Useful synthetic scenarios
- Readable launch story
